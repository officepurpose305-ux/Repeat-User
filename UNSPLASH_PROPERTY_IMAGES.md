## Tips for Extracting URLs

1. Go to unsplash.com
2. Search for your keyword
3. Click on an image you like
4. Right-click the image → "Copy image address"
5. The URL will be in this format: `https://images.unsplash.com/photo-XXXXXXXXXX`
6. Add the sizing params: `?w=800&h=600&fit=crop`
